World Championships

As I previously mentioned in the Copper's section, World’s was my favourite cheer competition of all time, my whole team flew out to Florida, we stayed on an amazing resort in houses together, and we had the experience of a lifetime. In order to get to Worlds, we needed a bid, which is essentially an invitation, where a small competition here, sponsors you to represent them at World’s. Coppers got this opportunity at a competition in Mississauga Ontario. It was a two day competition, where we hit two flawless routines, we ended up not only beating our biggest competitors, but also getting our bid to World’s, the feeling of this day was indescribable. We prepared day in and day out at every practice, to be ready for World’s in April. When we got to Florida, we got to practice in a very famous Florida Cheer Gym called Top Gun Allstars. We also practiced outside on the famous ESPN football field, and even in a practice gym that the competition sets up. These are all the iconic places that you dream of going when you hope to someday compete at World’s, so even this part of the experience was amazing. A few days later it was our day to compete, and we did, we hit a perfect 0 deduction routine, one of the best we have ever hit. This ended up landing us a spot on day 2 of the finals, where only the top 3 teams from each country got to move on, that alone was a huge accomplishment. The second day was not smooth sailing and we experienced some problems in our pyramid, but regardless of the unfortunate events, we still placed 7th and that was a huge accomplishment for us. We then got to spend the rest of the time in Disney and hanging out with our team at our resort. Overall, the entire experience of World’s is one I will never forget, ultimately making it my favourite competition.
